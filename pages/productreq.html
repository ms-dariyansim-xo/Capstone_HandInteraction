Product Requirments

Things to include:

ROLES AND RESPONSIBLLITIES: Product Owner, team members and stakeholders.

TEAM GOALS AND BUSINESS OBJECTIVES: Define most imp. goals in short point form.

BACKGROUND AND STRATEGIC FIT: Brieft explanation of strateic aim of actions. Why build the product? How actions affect prod. developments and align with company's goals?

ASSUMPTIONS: List of technical or business assumptions.

USER STORIES: List or link user stories that are required for proj. User story is a doc written from POV of person using your software prod. [use theories?]

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA: Conditions that indicate a user story is completed.

USER INTERACTION AND DESIGN: Link the design explorations and wireframes to page.

QUESTIONS: Record all questions and track them. [use forms and prev. history below content. ?]

NOT DOING: List things which aren't doing now but plan to.
